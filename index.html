Hey, what's up!

I'm Ammar, a story lover, runner, and computer science major attending Stony Brook University. Currently a junior that is expected to graduate in the month of May of 2021, I am actively learning about the different areas of computer science that I might want to explore further post college. What's most interesting to me right now is full stack web development, but other interests of mine also include computer networks, distributed systems, and data science.

Why Computer Science?

I wasn't always interested in technology. When I was young, I wanted to major in finance and work in the financial services industry, but much of that changed when I looked at some of the world's largest companies by market capitalization. Large corporations such as Apple, Facebook, Google, and Amazon as well as startups such as Tesla and Uber, companies that I have seen form and develop throughout my lifetime, made me realize that so much progress in improving the quality of life of common, everyday individuals have been the result of those who successfully created applications that have been engineered to work at an enormous scale. After attending Stony Brook University and learning about software development in the last few years, I knew I wanted to be in tech to work on complex, challenging problems that have real applications to the world.
